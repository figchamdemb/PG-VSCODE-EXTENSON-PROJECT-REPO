Current truth of the product: Stripe is used in payment mode, so these should be one-time prices representing one year of access. True recurring or automatic per-seat billing would require a backend checkout-model change.

Charges are set in GBP. International cards can still pay, and your bank or card provider may convert the final amount into local currency on your statement.

Referral and affiliate codes can also be used as a promotion path: buyers get the better of £2.50 off or 10% off, referral-code owners earn at least £2.00 per paid buyer, affiliate-code owners earn at least £2.50 per paid buyer, and the commission rate reaches 15% after 3 paid buyers.

Full refund stays open for the first 5 days. Referral and affiliate payouts are intentionally held until that refund window closes to reduce fraud.

Enterprise Custom

Need more seats or bigger Memory-bank quotas?

Enterprise Custom is the safe expansion path when a standard Enterprise package is too small. We agree the seat, device, and project caps first, bill it as a custom quote, invoice, or manual Stripe invoice, then activate the final limits from the admin portal.

Standard Enterprise

Public self-serve pricing stays on the normal Enterprise SKU map.

Current standard limit: 50 devices per licensed user and 2,000 Memory-bank projects.

Enterprise Custom Quote

Use this when you need higher seat counts, custom team sizing, or larger Memory-bank quotas.

Typical shape: larger seat caps with manually agreed project limits instead of unlimited access.

Activation Model

No extra public Stripe SKU is required.

After payment, the team or subscription is activated through existing admin manual-grant flows.
